Popular Topics

Secure / Non Secure (Blocked) Content and Videos
Each of the most popular Browsers (Firefox, IE, Chrome) displays content differently. Click the link above to learn how to display content in Sakai which may have been blocked by your browser.

Missing a Course? (More… drop-down menu)
Sakai displays the most recent courses you have visited. If you are expecting to see a course in your top menu bar, click the More… drop-down menu to see courses there isn’t room to display on your screen. If you are a student, check with your instructor that they have created a course site.

Sakai 10 Collapsible Menu (Left Menu)
Sakai 10 provides a collapsible left menu to increase your usable screen space. When collapsed, the menu displays as a series of icons. When expanded, the full menu displays.

Sakai 10 IM Chat
Sakai 10 now includes an Instant Message Chat feature. This document will explain how to find students and instructors when they are online.

Hide Old Course Sites
After using Sakai for several semesters, you may find that you have too many tabs displaying for course sites. This document will explain how to hide old course sites that you may not need to access on a regular basis.

Assign TA to Course Site
Teaching Assistants should be assigned the Teaching Assistant role within the course site. Assigning TAs as Instructors may be a FERPA violation. Adding a TA to a section they are not working with may also be a FERPA violation.
