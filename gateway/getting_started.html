FACULTY

Faculty Quick Start Guide for Sakai 2.9

Faculty, looking for your course in Sakai? Set up your course using the Worksite Setup tool in the My Workspace Area. See how.

Need Help with Sakai? Faculty can browse a collection of Sakai tutorials in the IS Knowledge Base or search Sakai's Help page.

Need training on Sakai? Faculty and staff can fill out our online request form and look for workshops scheduled through the Professional Development Center.

Need one-on-one help? Faculty should contact their ITG. Staff should contact the IS Service Desk. Students should contact The Bridge.

STUDENTS

Student Quick Start Guide for Sakai 2.9

Looking for your course in Sakai? Check with your instructor to verify he or she has set up a course site. Don't forget to check the "more" drop-down for additional sites.

Secure / Nonsecure Content in Sakai When your instructor provides a link to a website or file that is not housed on the Sakai server, your browser will block the content in an attempt to keep your computer safe. You can likely trust the content your instructor has provided for you within the Sakai course. Click here to learn how to unblock or allow this content to display in your preferred browser.

Need to configure your browser for Sakai? Sakai works best when your browser is set to trust content and pop-ups coming from Sakai.WFU.edu.

Need to hide old courses from past terms? You can choose which courses display on My Workspace by adjusting your settings under Preferences. Click here for a tutorial.

Need one-on-one help? Students should contact The Bridge.
