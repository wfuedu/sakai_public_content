News and Information

Sakai has now been upgraded to version 2.9.In addition to bug fixes, Sakai now has new functionality. New features include an improved Profile tool and a more robust content editor with added math capabilities. New tools include an Assignments tool that better integrates with the Gradebook and an additional Gradebook tool with more flexibility.

Reminder: Sakai works best with Mozilla Firefox.

Getting Started

Faculty, looking for your course in Sakai? Set up your course using the Worksite Setup tool in the My Workspace Area. See how.

Students, looking for your course in Sakai? Check with your insturctor to verify he or she has set up a course site. Don't forget to check the "more" drop-down for additional sites.

Need training on Sakai? Faculty and staff can fill out our online request form and look for workshops scheduled through the Professional Development Center.

Need one-on-one help? Faclulty should contact their ITG. Staff should contact the IS Service Desk. Students should contact The Bridge.
